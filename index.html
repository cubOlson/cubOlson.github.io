About Me

I was originally introduced to coding in college while working on my Art Technology degree. We were getting into 3D modeling and my professor showed me how to do some procedural generation with Python inside of Cinema 4D. I also picked up some C# and used it to create basic animations on a small scale. After graduation I worked as an axe-throwing coach in Colorado and started up my own small animation business working with the Adobe Creative Suite. During the pandemic I changed careers and got into software engineering - I can now build fullstack websites in both Javascript and Python and add to my skills every day.
